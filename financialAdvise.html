Financial Model

Lecturers

At the University of Cape tutors get paid between a R109 (for undergraduate students) and R193 (PhD graduate) a/hour at the time that I am reading this.

https://hr.uct.ac.za/remuneration-benefits-remuneration/rates-tutors-demonstrators-teaching- assistants-and-guest-lecturersteachers-2023

Our goal with regards to lecturer payment is to be somewhere in the middle of this. R 150/hour would be a good target.

One of the problems with tutoring is that there is hardly any flexibility in terms of when you will be presenting a class.Instead of having anyone tell you when you must present a class, you organize the class times yourself, be reasonable. Learners do not want to be at school until 6.

Business Expander

The business expander is a lecturer, but in addition to being a lecture also expands our business. They would get other schools and or communities to join the school system. We strongly encourage you to be a business expander. The bonus could be a % revenue or a once of cash bonus. Saying exactly what the reward will be is hard to say, because it will depend on the situation. For example:

  1. How many learners did Business Expander get to join?
  2. How long will this community join for?
  3. Is the school in a high theft area?
  4. The list goes on.
